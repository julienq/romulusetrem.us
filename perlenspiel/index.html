Perlenspiel

Some experiments made with Brian Moriarty's Perlenspiel. Works best in Firefox.

  • Atlas: puzzle game where you need to flip every tile to the same color. Your position is shown by a green outline; you can move to adjacent tiles by clicking them. Entering a tile will flip its color.
  • Picopolis: this will remind you of a very famous game... Choose tools with the following keys: R for residential, C for commercial, and I for industrial zoning; S for streets. This is very much in progress!
  • Xylophone: a simple sequencer; toggle cells to play or mute notes; use up/down arrow key to change the tempo.
  • Colors: a simple color toy; move the mouse and the scroll wheel.
